# High School Feeder Program Review

A review of US high schools that produce professional athletes (NBA, NFL, MLB, NHL) identified additional feeder schools not previously in the database.

## Sources

- Pro Football Reference high school rankings
- MaxPreps (NBA, MLB feeder lists)
- BroBible, SportsCasting, Stadium Talk (NFL/MLB top producers)
- Baseball-Reference, The Baseball Cube (MLB)
